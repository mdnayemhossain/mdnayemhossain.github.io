<h2 class="quote-title">About Me</h2>

I'm a graduate student in [Electronics](https://www.oulu.fi/en/) at the University of Oulu, Finland. Previously, I worked as an Analog Layout Design Engineer at [Quest Global](https://www.quest-global.com/) and an Assistant Engineer at [Ulkasemi](https://www.ulkasemi.com/), and earlier as a research student in the [NSU Optics Lab](https://sites.google.com/view/nsuopticslab/home) at North South University.

My interests lie in VLSI, photonics, and quantum computing - pushing electronic design beyond the limits of Moore's law. Outside of work, I volunteer for social causes and enjoy reading thrillers, mysteries, fantasy, science fiction, and classics.
